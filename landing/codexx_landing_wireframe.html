The engine
Custom AST front-end. Forked tree-sitter, brought to C++23, with a Prosser-based preprocessor for macro robustness and a full CST→AST transformation. No Clang dependency — no legacy baggage. The custom CFG enables inter-procedural and thread-aware analysis unavailable in existing tools.
